Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a workshop? ▾
Browse our workshops section, click "Enroll Now", and complete the registration form. Payment plans are available for multi-week programs.
What qualifications do I need? ▾
Requirements vary by program. Each workshop page clearly states prerequisites. Many programs are open to anyone with a background in the relevant field.
Are certificates internationally recognised? ▾
Yes. Our certificates are recognised by 200+ partner institutions worldwide. They include a digital verification code for authentication.
Is there 24/7 technical support? ▾
Our technical support team is available 24/7 via email and live chat for enrolled participants. General enquiries are handled Mon–Fri.
Can institutions partner with GRC? ▾
Absolutely. We actively seek partnerships with universities and research institutions. Use the contact form above selecting "Partnership" to start the conversation.
